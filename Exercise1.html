Overgeared Synopsis

Author: Park Saenal (박새날)


Shin Youngwoo has had an unfortunate life and is now stuck carrying bricks on construction sites.
He even had to do labor in the VR game, Satisfy! However, luck would soon enter his hapless life.
His weak character, 'Grid', would soon discover the dangerous Northern End Cave for a perilous quest,
and in that dark and shady place, he would find 'Pagma's Book'
and become the only legendary class player…
